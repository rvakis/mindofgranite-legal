Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

1. Overview

Mind of Granite ("App") is an automated content publishing tool. This policy describes how we handle data.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data from third-party users. The App is used solely by its owner to publish content to their own TikTok account.

3. TikTok API Usage

The App uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish video content. The only data accessed is the App owner's TikTok account for the purpose of uploading videos. No user data from TikTok viewers or followers is collected.

4. Data Storage

API credentials are stored securely on a private server. No user data is stored in databases or shared with third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.

6. Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact the App administrator.
